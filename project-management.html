property management.

at bamboo property management (BPM) we provide consulting services to the real estate industry, financial institutions, and the private and public sectors in nigeria and across other parts of africa.

our strong presence combined with a profound knowledge of the africa property market gives us an edge in identifying unique development opportunities that fit into our development and investment profile by leveraging on our experience garnered through the years in the service delivery chain.

the scope of business includes acquisition, development, sales, lease and management of real estate properties, facilities management; valuation of real estate, fixed or movable assets of plant and machinery; and development feasibility study. we also offer comprehensive consultancy services to all sectors in the industry.

we are organized with a qualified team and comprehensive real estate database to respond effectively to any request, placing our customers’ care at the core of our business culture.
